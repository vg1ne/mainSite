A brand is a candy paper wrap — the exterior of your business. It is the first thing your potential customer visualy sees. The significance of branding can’t be overemphasized: we won’t buy a car which we don’t like and we don’t care how many horses there are under the hood. We won’t go to the restaurant, the name of which doesn’t get up an appetite and we won’t go to a movie if its trailer is boring However, the car which your ignored may actually be very reliable and durable, the «tasteless» named restaurant may offer the most exquisite dishes, and the movie with a boring trailer may turn out to be a masterpiece of a young director. That’s why the brand is so important.
